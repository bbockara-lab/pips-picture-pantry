# Pip's Picture Pantry Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-18

Pip's Picture Pantry is a cozy picture puzzle game by Sunny Spoon Studios.

## Information We Collect

Pip's Picture Pantry does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

The game stores limited gameplay information on your device only, such as:

- Puzzle progress
- Completed cards
- In-game spoon balance
- Unlocked stages
- Optional player nickname
- Language and sound settings

This information is stored locally on your device and is not sent to Sunny Spoon Studios or any external server.

## Children

Pip's Picture Pantry is designed as a gentle puzzle game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Advertising and Analytics

Pip's Picture Pantry does not include third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or tracking SDKs.

## In-App Purchases

Pip's Picture Pantry includes optional in-app support purchases for app-store processed spoon support and top-up items. Payments are processed by the official app store platform. The app does not collect, store, or receive your payment card details. Store product identifiers for release tracking: `pip_cozy_support`, `pip_spoon_jar_small`.

## Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties.

## Data Deletion

Because gameplay data is stored only on your device, you can delete it by using the reset option in the app or by uninstalling the app.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app changes. The updated policy will be posted at the same public URL.
