# Elena's Cozy Village - App Icon Brief

Last draft update: June 8, 2026

## Goal

Create an original, child-friendly icon that communicates cozy story cooking, Elena, and Pip without resembling any reference restaurant-management game.

## Recommended Direction

Primary icon concept:
- Pip the capybara holding a small recipe card beside a warm soup bowl.
- Elena's red/pink helper accent or chef hat detail appears as a small supporting shape.
- Sunny Spoon Cafe warmth is communicated through a spoon, soup steam, and sticker-like outline.

Why this works:
- Pip is the most distinctive mascot.
- The soup/recipe card ties directly to Day 1 and the game's learning-through-action loop.
- The icon can remain readable at small App Store sizes.

## Visual Rules

Use:
- Original illustrated sticker style.
- Cream or white sticker outline.
- Warm cafe palette with rose, honey yellow, soft teal, and soup orange.
- Big readable Pip face.
- One clear object: recipe card or soup bowl.

Avoid:
- PlateUp-like kitchen grids, counters, or restaurant-management UI.
- Text in the icon.
- Too many tiny props.
- Generic animal cafe branding.
- Full cast composition that becomes unreadable at small sizes.

## Required Export Targets

Final icon production should prepare:
- 1024 x 1024 App Store icon.
- iOS app icon set via the packaging pipeline.
- Android adaptive icon foreground/background if Google Play is prepared.

## Open Items

- Final generated or illustrated icon artwork.
- Small-size readability check.
- Similarity audit against early visual references.
- Transparent-source or editable master asset retained for future updates.
